Tips for efficient workflows

  • Use 1-9 to quickly select persons by number, then C+F/P/N to set consent
  • Press P to toggle preview while adjusting mask settings to see changes in real-time
  • Use J/K/L for video navigation (like YouTube) - familiar for most users
  • Frame-by-frame navigation (, and .) helps verify masking at transition points
