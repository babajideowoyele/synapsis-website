The Challenge

Privacy barriers limit data sharing

SSH researchers increasingly rely on audiovisual data to study complex human behaviours, but privacy concerns prevent sharing and reuse of this valuable data.

Current masking tools are perceived as too technical, making it difficult for researchers to adopt privacy-preserving practices.

Our Solution

Platform + Training

SYNAPSIS integrates the MaskAnyone toolkit into a user-friendly platform with SURF cloud infrastructure, making masking accessible to all researchers.

Our Masking School workshops train researchers and data stewards, building digital competence across Dutch universities.

Capabilities

What SYNAPSIS offers

Face Masking

Blur, pixelate, or replace faces while preserving research-relevant features.

Voice Masking

Alter voice characteristics to prevent speaker identification.

Body Kinematics

Extract pose data and movement analytics from masked videos.

Cloud Platform

SURF-integrated infrastructure for secure, scalable processing.

Training

Masking School workshops for researchers and data stewards.

GDPR Compliance

Tools and guidelines for privacy-compliant data sharing.
