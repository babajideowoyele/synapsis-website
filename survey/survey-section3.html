Section 3: Masking & De-identification

This section explores your experience and needs regarding privacy-preserving techniques for audiovisual data.

What is Masking/De-identification?

Masking refers to techniques that remove or obscure personally identifiable information from audiovisual data (like faces, voices, or background details) while preserving research-relevant features like gestures, prosody, or interaction patterns. This enables GDPR-compliant data sharing.

👤
Face Masking
Blur, pixelate, or replace faces
🎤
Voice Alteration
Pitch shift or voice synthesis
🖼️
Background
Remove or blur backgrounds
